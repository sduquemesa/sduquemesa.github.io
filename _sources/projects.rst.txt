Generative Sound

* `Sound experiments with an epidemiological model of coronavirus <https://youtu.be/ez1iTJf8cKY?si=oAjIVlAhiRm4WVAF&t=218>`_

   Eurorack modular + quantum computer: a trigger sequencer generated by the outcomes of one of Xanadu's X-series quantum hardware.

    A cellular automata generates a MIDI score that is played by several digital instruments.
